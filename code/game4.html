How to Play

  • Use your mouse or touch to move the water container at the bottom
  • Catch the blue raindrops to fill your container and earn points
  • Watch out! There's a 10% chance each raindrop causes a leak
  • Leaks appear at random positions on your bucket
  • Leaks reduce your water level by 1% each second
  • Fill your bucket to 80% to win before time runs out!
  • Avoid missing raindrops - you lose a life for each one that hits the ground
