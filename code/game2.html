How to Play

  • Use your mouse or touch to move the water container at the bottom
  • Catch the blue raindrops to fill your container and earn points
  • Watch the water level rise in your container and the indicator on the right
  • Avoid missing raindrops - you lose a life for each one that hits the ground
  • Game gets faster as you progress to higher levels
  • You have 60 seconds to collect as much water as possible!
